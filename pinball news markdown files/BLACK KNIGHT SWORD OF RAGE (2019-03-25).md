Stern Pinball today released a teaser video revealing the title of designer Steve Ritchie’s next game.

Until now universally referred to as *Black Knight 3000* as the follow-up to his 1989 title *Black Knight 2000*, the actual name will be *Black Knight: Sword of Rage*.
