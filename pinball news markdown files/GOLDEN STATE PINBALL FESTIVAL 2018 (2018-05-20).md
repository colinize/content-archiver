**Date:**
18th-20th May, 2018

**Location:**
Lodi Grape Festival Fairgrounds, 413 E. Lockeford Street, Lodi, California 95240, USA

Welcome to our coverage of the very first Golden State Pinball Festival which is being held in the city of Lodi in northern California.
