**Date:**
17th - 20th October, 2018
**Location:** Westin Chicago North Shore, 601 North Milwaukee Avenue, Wheeling, IL 60090, USA

Hello and welcome to our extensive coverage of the [34th annual Pinball Expo](http://pinballexpo.com/) which is being held in the familiar surroundings of the Westin Chicago North Shore in the city of Wheeling near Chicago.

[![The Pinball Expo 2018 logo](https://www.pinballnews.com/site/wp-content/uploads/shows/pinball-expo-2018/001-pinball-expo-2018.png)](https://www.pinballnews.com/site/wp-content/uploads/shows/pinball-expo-2018/001-pinball-expo-2018.png)

We will be covering the events across all five days, starting with the Bumper Blast welcome party on Wednesday evening and continuing through all the seminars, the games hall, the vendor hall, the Stern Pinball factory tour, and the multiple tournaments.

We will be dividing up our reports so each day has its own article.  That means you can get at all the latest news more easily and we can update those reports as frequently as possible.  So here are our articles so far:

[**Click here**](https://www.pinballnews.com/site/2018/10/18/pinball-expo-2018-wednesday/) – for Wednesday’s report with the overview, our special preview and the Bumper Blast welcome party.

[**Click here**](https://www.pinballnews.com/site/2018/10/18/pinball-expo-2018-thursday/) – for Thursday’s report with the Stern Pinball factory tour and the start of the seminars.

[**Click here**](https://www.pinballnews.com/site/2018/10/19/pinball-expo-2018-friday/) – for Friday’s report with a full day of seminars and the Hall of Fame inductions.

[**Click here**](https://www.pinballnews.com/site/2018/10/20/pinball-expo-2018-saturday/) – for Saturday’s report with a final full day of seminars and a look at the first Games Hall.

[**Click here**](https://www.pinballnews.com/site/2018/10/21/pinball-expo-2018-sunday/) – for Sunday’s report with a look at the second Games Hall, the Vendor Hall and the tournament finals.

Keep checking here at Pinball News for all the latest news and events from Wheeling and the 34th annual Pinball Expo.
