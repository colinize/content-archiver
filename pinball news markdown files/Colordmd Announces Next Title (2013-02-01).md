Date: 21st January, 2013

The fourth title in the ColorDMD range has been announced.

After their debut with Attack from Mars and the subsequent Medieval Madness and The Addams Family,
the next title is to be Star Trek - The Next Generation.

They have produced a promotional video to accompany the release which is expected to be available mid-late February 2013.

The Star Trek - The Next Generation ColorDMD will be available from the usual distributors.
