For many pinball fans, the name of Ryan Claytor only appeared on their radar thanks to his 2019 collaboration with Nick Baldridge to create the first edition of the [Coin-Op Carnival](https://www.pinballnews.com/site/2019/03/07/coin-op-carnival/) comic book series.

However, Ryan has been involved in both pinball and comic books for far longer, and today the [Elephant Eater Comics](http://elephanteater.com/) brand he created is celebrating twenty years of publishing on-line and in-print with the launch of a new book and associated [Kickstarter campaign](https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/ryanclaytor/one-bite-at-a-time) to help fund it.
